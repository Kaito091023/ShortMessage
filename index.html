My tinatangi

   She's beautiful, but not like those girls in the magazines. She's beautiful, for the way she thought. She's beautiful, just like the sparkle in her eyes when she talked about something she loves. She's beautiful, for her ability to make other people smile, even if she was the complete opposite. No, she wasn't beautiful for something as temporary as her looks. She's beautiful, not just her face but also deep down to her soul.

   Even if loving you felt like a book out in the wind, even if the pages turned too fast for me to read, I'll still choose you. Even if I didn't get the chance to adore you, to trace your lines with my fingertip and re-read my favorite parts, I'll still choose you. To live the story I know we're meant to create together, where each chapter unfolds with new adventures and joy. I look forward to the many pages yet to be written in our beautiful tale, tangi.
